About Elections

Voter Registration: Eligibility: Citizens aged 18 and above are eligible to vote. Enrollment: Citizens need to register themselves as voters by submitting Form 6 to the Electoral Registration Officer (ERO) or online through the National Voter's Service Portal (NVSP). Voter ID: Once registered, voters receive a Voter ID card (Electoral Photo Identity Card - EPIC) containing their details and a unique voter identification number. Polling Day Procedures: Polling Booths: Voters cast their votes at designated polling stations allocated based on their residential address. Identification: Voters present their EPIC or any approved identification document to the polling officials for verification. Voting Method: Voters proceed to the Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) where they cast their vote by pressing the button next to the candidate or symbol of their choice. In some cases, paper ballots are used.
